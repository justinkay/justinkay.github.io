Justin Kay

I am the Co-Founder and CTO of Ai.Fish. We work on computer vision and machine learning applications in sustainable fisheries management and marine conservation.

I am also a researcher in the Computatational Vision Lab at Caltech, where I work with Pietro Perona, Sara Beery, and Grant Van Horn on computer vision methods for biodiversity monitoring.
